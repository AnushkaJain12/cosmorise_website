LVM 3D1

LVM 3D1 is an Earth observation satellite by ISRO, used for applications like agriculture and disaster management.

CRYOGENIC UPPER STAGE

A cryogenic upper stage is a rocket stage that uses cryogenic fuels (liquid oxygen and liquid hydrogen) for propulsion. It offers higher efficiency and greater performance, enabling spacecraft to reach higher orbits or distant destinations.

CORE LIQUID BOOSTERS

Core Liquid Boosters are the main liquid-fueled stages of a launch vehicle, providing primary thrust during liftoff and ascent. In rockets these boosters use liquid propellants (typically liquid oxygen and kerosene) to propel the rocket through the atmosphere.

STRAP ON BOOSTERS

In LVM 3D1 (GSLV Mk III-D1), strap-on boosters are solid-fuel boosters attached to the main core, providing extra thrust during liftoff. They are jettisoned after their fuel is spent.
